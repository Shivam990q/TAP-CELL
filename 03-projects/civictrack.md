# CivicTrack — Civic Issue Management Platform

- One-liner: Platform that lets citizens report local issues (road damage, garbage, water
  leaks) and track their resolution.
- Status: Full-stack live project
- Links: Repo: github.com/Shivam990q/CivicTrack (also: civic-zone-connect)
- Timeline: ~2025
- Role: Full-stack developer

## Problem
Citizens lack an easy way to report and track local civic issues and engage with their
community.

## Tech stack
React (frontend) + Django (backend)

## What I built (key features)
- Issue reporting (road damage, garbage, water leaks, etc.)
- Resolution tracking
- Community engagement features
