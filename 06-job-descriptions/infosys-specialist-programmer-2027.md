## Hiring process
1. **Online programming ability test** — 3 coding questions, **180 minutes**. DSA, medium–hard.
   (Question switching allowed; partial scoring by test cases; proctored via Infosys Wingspan.)
2. **In-depth technical interview** (~60 min) for those who clear the test.

## Test details (from public experiences — GeeksforGeeks/Medium)
- 3 DSA problems (easy→hard mix), topics: **Dynamic Programming, Greedy, Graphs, Arrays/Strings,
  Bit Manipulation, Recursion/Backtracking, Math, Hashing**. Constraints are high (need efficient sol).
- Solving ~2–3 well (passing majority of test cases) typically earns an interview call.
- Competitive-programming style; strong DSA is essential for the SP tiers.

## Technical interview (from experiences)
- Deep DSA (approach + code + complexity), sometimes on paper/whiteboard.
- CS fundamentals: **DBMS/SQL, OOP, Operating Systems, Computer Networks**.
- Project discussion (they probe your resume: NETWORKED, Parakh, etc.).
- Sometimes puzzles/aptitude; "why Infosys"; willingness to relocate & learn any tech.
